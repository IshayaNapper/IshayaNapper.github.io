Hi, my name is I’shaya Napper I am a mother and junior developer. I’ve been coding for about a year and I am falling in love with it more each day.
I first was introduced to coding via my college instructor. I had never seen any code before. I had always had an interest in computers stemming from myspace days, where we could customize our profile lol!
Once I began building projects, I knew this was the industry for me. I am exciting to join a growing industry and become the first Black tech professional in my family. I am not the common person for this field which makes me more passionate. I would love to show my daughter anything is possible with persistence and determination. Outside of coding I do enjoy, working out, meditation, trying new restaurants or just spending time at home.
